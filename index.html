You can calculate your max lift, warmup sets, and also estimate your current weightlifting level! There are various Youtube videos including how-to videos and it will recommend routines based on your level. First, start off by finding out your weightlifting level using the calculator on the right!
(if you don't know what your 1 rep max weight is, please use the calculator in Tools page to find it out.)
